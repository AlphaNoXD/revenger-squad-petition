About the Petition

The Revenger Squad is advocating for balanced homework loads for Filipino students that prioritize mental health and well-being. Excessive homework can lead to burnout, anxiety, and decreased academic performance, particularly in the unique educational landscape of the Philippines.

Why It Matters

  • 75% of Filipino students report feeling overwhelmed by homework.
  • Excessive homework can lead to anxiety and depression among students.
  • Balanced homework loads can improve academic performance and overall well-being.

Consequences of Homework Burnout

Homework burnout can have serious consequences on the mental health and academic success of Filipino students. Those experiencing burnout may face:

  • Decreased Motivation: A heavy homework load can lead to a lack of interest in learning and reduced motivation to complete assignments.
  • Physical Health Issues: Chronic stress from excessive homework can manifest in physical symptoms such as headaches, fatigue, and sleep disturbances.
  • Social Isolation: Students may withdraw from social activities to keep up with homework, impacting their relationships and overall well-being.
  • Lower Academic Performance: Ironically, the stress from too much homework can lead to lower grades, as students may struggle to retain information and manage their time effectively.

Potential Solutions

To address homework burnout among Filipino students, we propose several solutions:

  • Homework Guidelines: Schools should establish clear guidelines on the amount of homework assigned based on grade level and subject matter, taking into account the local context.
  • Flexible Deadlines: Allowing students to have flexible deadlines can reduce stress and help them manage their workload more effectively.
  • Incorporating Breaks: Encouraging students to take regular breaks during homework sessions can improve focus and productivity.
  • Parental Involvement: Parents should be encouraged to engage in discussions with their children about homework and its impact on their lives, fostering a supportive environment.

Who Supports This Petition?

The Revenger Squad has the backing of:

  • Educational Psychologists in the Philippines
  • Student Organizations
  • Parents and Guardians
  • Teachers advocating for student wellness

Purpose of the Petition

This petition aims to raise awareness about the detrimental effects of excessive homework on Filipino students' mental health and overall well-being. By gathering signatures and support, we hope to encourage educational institutions in the Philippines to reconsider their homework policies and prioritize a balanced approach that fosters learning without compromising students' health. Our goal is to create an educational environment where Filipino students can thrive academically while maintaining their mental and emotional well-being.
